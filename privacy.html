Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how OpenClaw collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our QuickBooks integration service.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks account, we access:

  • Purchase order data (numbers, amounts, vendor information)
  • Invoice data received via email
  • Company information (name, ID) for identification

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this data solely for:

  • Matching incoming invoices to existing purchase orders
  • Posting matched results to Teams for review
  • Improving invoice extraction accuracy
Important: OpenClaw operates with read-only access to QuickBooks. We cannot create, modify, or delete any data in your QuickBooks account.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Access tokens are encrypted and stored locally on secure infrastructure
  • Invoice data is processed in memory and not permanently stored
  • We use industry-standard TLS encryption for all data transmission

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your QuickBooks data to third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Your organization's OpenClaw administrator
  • Authorized team members in your Teams channel

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect OpenClaw from your QuickBooks account at any time
  • Request deletion of your data
  • View what data we have accessed

6. Retention

We retain invoice matching data only as long as necessary for processing. Once an invoice is matched and posted to Teams, the underlying data is discarded.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your OpenClaw administrator.
